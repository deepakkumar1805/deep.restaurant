What is the idli recipe ?

Idli is a soft , pillowy streamed savory cake made from fermented rice and lenti batter and the lentils used in making the idli batter are urad dal.

Ghee may be used as a condiment. Idli is among the most nutritious foods in india cuisine with rice plant-based proteins, fibre, complex carbohydrates and micronutrients. It has no fat and no cholesterol, making it a perfect heart health food. It's a fermented food that is beneficial for the gut health.

What is the Creamy Cottage Cheese ?

Creamy Cottage Cheese is the general term used to designate the fresh, soft, uncured, high-moisture cheese made usually from pasteurized sweet skin milk, or occasionally from either reconstituted nonfat dry milk or plain condensed skim milk.

While paneer is pressed and compacted to form a solid block, cottage cheese is typically sold in small curds or chunks.
